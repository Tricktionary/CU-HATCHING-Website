About Us

To schools large and small who want to improve student learning with an efficient, cheap, and easy-to-use service. Crater is an educational service that allows students to learn their school subjects while making an impact within their community. Unlike Kumon, Crater provides students with the support to create real life substantial projects in their community in order to improve and enhance their educational learning and experience in an engaging way.
